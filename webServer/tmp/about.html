The web search engine built for zambia

Cognifly's mission is to become the number one web search engine for zambian web searches. We are working very hard to build an engine that gives relevant results to your search queries. just type what you want in the search box and cognifly will present you with results and information from websites intended for zambians.

cognifly scopes

Cognifly is built on the idea of scopes. Scopes are rich user experinces that deliver relevant information and content based on whatever the user searched for. They feel, look and work far more efficiently than traditional links. scopes are a complete reinvention of content and service delivery. They focus on bringing content out.

Cognifly is built from scratch in Go

Go or golang is a new open source, general-purpose programming language. It is simple, compiled, statically typed, expressive, concise, clean, efficient, very productive and incorperates concurrency primitives that make it easy to write programs that get the most out of multicore CPUs and networked machines.
